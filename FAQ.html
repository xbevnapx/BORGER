FAQ

Q: What exactly is a borger?
A: A borger is an experience, a questionable life
choice. It’s a stackable masterpiece of ingredients you
didn’t know you needed, carefully engineered to reach
absurd heights—literally.

Q: How does the Endless Borger work?
A: Each package starts with a bun, patty, and a slice of
cheese. From there, stack on endless ingredients to
your heart's content! It’s a tower of entertainment.
a guaranteed conversation starter when guests see it
in your home. Every new layer adds flavor, flair, and
maybe a hint of instability to your life.

Q: What can I stack in my borger?
A: We’ve got it all—classic toppings with more colors
than a rainbow. Maybe even some questionable
ingredients. From marshmallow fluff to
glow-in-the-dark pickles, you can stack until physics
itself taps out.

Q: How high can I stack my borger?
A: We’re still testing the limits. So far, we’ve reached
the height of an average giraffe, but with enough
layers, you could rival Mount Everest.
We recommend a helmet after 10 feet.

Q: How do I order more layers?
A: Just click on ‘Add More Layers’ to summon fresh
ingredients directly to your doorstep.

Q: How do the pieces stay together?
A: Each layer uses cutting-edge borger-bond tech
for premium stackability. It’s engineered to withstand mild
tremors, light wind, and possibly the curiosity of
small pets.
Q: Are there limited-edition ingredients?
A: Absolutely! We release seasonal “rare layers” like
Pumpkin Spice Bacon and Unicorn Mayo. Collect them
all, or miss out and live with regret.
